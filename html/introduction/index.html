My Favorite Football Team: Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. It was founded in 1902 and is one of the most successful and popular football clubs in the world. The team is often called "Los Blancos" which means "The Whites" in Spanish.

🏆 Achievements

  • 35 La Liga titles
  • 20 Copa del Rey trophies
  • 15 UEFA Champions League titles (most in history)

🏆 Top 3 Champions League Wins

  1. 2014 — La Décima (10th)
    Ramos’s 93’ header forced extra time, Real Madrid won 4–1 vs Atlético Madrid.
  2. 2017 — Back-to-Back Era
    First club in UCL modern era to retain the title, defeating Juventus 4–1.
  3. 2022 — Comeback Champions
    Remontadas run ended with a 1–0 win over Liverpool in the final.
