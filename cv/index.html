About Me

Hello, I'm Rayhana, a multi-talented artist. My main interests include drawing and creating artistic content, as well as character writing. I began my journey in the world of drawing with a specific goal. to transform the characters I imagined as a writer into illustrated images that accurately reflect their identities. I have spent five years practicing the art of drawing, and I take pride in showcasing my work in my personal portfolio.
