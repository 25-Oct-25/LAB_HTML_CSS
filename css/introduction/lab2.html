Al Hilal Saudi Club

"The Leader" — One of the most successful clubs in Saudi Arabia and Asia.

About the Club

Al Hilal Club was founded in Riyadh in 1957. It is the most decorated Saudi club, winning many local and Asian titles. The team is nicknamed The Leader due to its dominance in championships.

Main Achievements:

  • Saudi Professional League Championships.
  • King’s Cup Titles.
  • AFC Champions League Titles.

Star Player:

Salem Al-Dawsari — One of Al Hilal’s most iconic players, famous for scoring decisive goals in local and international tournaments.
