What Is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that creates systems to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving. These systems are powered by algorithms, often using techniques like machine learning and deep learning, to analyze data, make predictions, and improve their performance over time without being explicitly programmed for every scenario. Everyday examples include voice assistants, search engine suggestions, and self-driving cars.

The Evolution of AI

Artificial Intelligence has come a long way since its inception. From early rule-based systems to deep learning and generative models, AI now powers virtual assistants, personalized recommendations, healthcare, autonomous vehicles, and creative works. Future AI promises seamless integration into daily life, enhancing productivity, creativity, and decision-making, while raising ethical and governance challenges.
