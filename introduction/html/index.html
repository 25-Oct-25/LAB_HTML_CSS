About Coffee

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain Coffea species. It is one of the most popular drinks worldwide and can be enjoyed in various forms.

Types of Coffee

  • Espresso
  • Latte
  • Cappuccino
  • Americano

Coffee Beans Varieties

  1. Arabica
  2. Robusta
  3. Liberica
  4. Excelsa

Brewing Methods

There are several methods to brew coffee, including:

  1. Drip Coffee Maker
  2. French Press
  3. Espresso Machine
  4. Aeropress
  5. Cold Brew
