Graduation Project

Vision for the Blind

One of my greatest achievements was my graduation project, "Vision for the Blind", an intelligent assistive system designed to help visually impaired individuals recognize the people around them.

The system uses a Reinforcement Learning method to capture and analyze images of people standing in front of the user, identify them, and then provide audio feedback announcing their names.

Later, the project was enhanced into a portable device that allows blind users to recognize people in real time — making it a practical and innovative solution that improves accessibility and independence.
